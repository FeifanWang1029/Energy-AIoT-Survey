# Publication Trends

*Last updated: 2025-03-02*

## By year

Most papers in the current collection are from **2022–2025**, with a strong concentration in **2025** (carbon-aware scheduling, carbon-aware inference, and edge/cross-layer energy). Earlier years (2019–2020) contribute offloading and MEC energy work.

## By venue

- **arXiv** dominates the initial set (preprints / early versions); many later appear at NSDI, SIGCOMM, OpenReview (ICLR), IEEE, etc.
- **Top published venues** so far: NSDI (GREEN), SIGCOMM (PCAPS), IEEE TMC (MobiSplit), OpenReview (CarbonGearRL), TinyML/CVPR/MLCommons for device-level and benchmarks.
- **Systems conferences** (NSDI, OSDI, EuroSys, MobiCom, SenSys) and **ML systems** (MLSys) will be added as more papers are collected.

## Emerging topics

- **Carbon-aware scheduling and inference** — Multiple 2025 works (GREEN, EcoServe, CarbonGearRL, CarbonFlex, PCAPS) focus on reducing CO₂ for training and inference.
- **Edge and cross-layer energy** — Integrated sensing–communication–computation (ISCC), mobility-aware partitioning (MobiSplit), and dynamic offloading (Infer-EDGE, EEDTO).
- **TinyML and device-level energy** — TinyMAN, TinyOps, MinUn, ultralow-power IoT devices; MLPerf Tiny and MLPerf Power as benchmarks.
